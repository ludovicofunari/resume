ABOUT

Ludovico Funari is a Ph.D. student at the University of Rome Tor Vergata. He received the master's degree in “ICT And Internet Engineering” in October 2019. His research activity includes IoT, Cloud and Edge computing. He has worked in European projects such as EU H2020 “Fed4IoT” project as a CNIT researcher and on MIUR research “Liquid Edge” at the University of Rome Tor Vergata. In 2021 he worked on a GEANT project named PLAS (Platformed Workflows). His Kubernetes knowledge is certified with both CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator) and CKAD (Certified Kubernetes Application Developer).
